# Car Insurance System - Activity Diagram

## Overview
This diagram shows the process of handling an insurance claim step by step.

## Process

1. The process starts when a claim is submitted.
2. The system verifies the insurance policy.
3. If the policy is not valid, the claim is rejected.
4. If the policy is valid:
   - The vehicle damage is inspected.
   - The repair cost is estimated.
5. The system checks the type of claim:
   - For major claims, a detailed assessment is done.
   - For minor claims, a fast-track approval is used.
6. The claim is approved.
7. The system continues with:
   - Arranging repair service
   - Notifying the customer
8. After that:
   - The payout is processed
   - The claim is closed

## Alternative Flow

- The claim can be cancelled before the payout is processed.

## Conclusion

This diagram helps to understand the full workflow of an insurance claim, from submission to completion.
